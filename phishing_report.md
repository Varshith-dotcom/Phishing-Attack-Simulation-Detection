# Phishing Attack Simulation Report

## 1. What is Phishing?
Phishing is a social engineering attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information by impersonating trusted entities.

## 2. Types of Phishing
- Email Phishing
- Spear Phishing
- Smishing (SMS phishing)
- Vishing (Voice phishing)

## 3. Phishing Simulation
Sample phishing emails and URLs were analyzed to observe common attack patterns such as fake domains, urgent messages, and misleading links.

## 4. Indicators of Phishing
- Suspicious sender email address
- Fake website URLs
- Urgent or threatening language
- Grammar and spelling mistakes
- Unexpected attachments

## 5. Detection Techniques
- URL scanning
- Email header analysis
- Domain reputation checking
- User awareness

## 6. Prevention Methods
- Verify sender identity
- Avoid clicking unknown links
- Use email filters
- Enable multi-factor authentication

## 7. Conclusion
Phishing awareness is critical in preventing social engineering attacks and protecting sensitive information.
